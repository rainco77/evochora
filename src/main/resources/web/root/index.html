EVOCHORA
Welcome to Evochora
Evochora is a collaborative platform for research into the foundational physics of digital evolution. It provides a rich, n-dimensional environment where organisms are embodied agents that must navigate their world, actively forage for energy, and solve the mechanical, metabolic, and ecological challenges of self-replication — all using EvoASM as its low-level assembly language, making their behaviors fully evolvable.

You are viewing an Evochora node instance. It may be the public demo / reference deployment or a self-hosted installation on your own machine or cluster. For running your own long-term experiments or customized setups, you need to operate your own Evochora node. The complete source code and setup instructions are available on GitHub.
