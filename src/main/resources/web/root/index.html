EVOCHORA
Welcome to Evochora
Evochora is a collaborative platform for research into the foundational physics of digital evolution. It provides a rich, n-dimensional environment where organisms are embodied agents that must navigate their world, actively forage for energy, and solve the mechanical, metabolic, and ecological challenges of self-replication—all using a low-level assembly language, making their behaviors fully evolvable.
